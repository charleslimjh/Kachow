About Us

Bukit Batok Driving Centre is a leading driving centre in Singapore.

We offer comprehensive and systematic driving and riding courses to individuals and qualified drivers. We also provide defensive driving and riding programmes for local organisations as well as conduct courses overseas such as China, Vietnam, Hungary, Malaysia and Philippines. As part of our social contributions, we provide road safety talks and demonstrations to kindergartens, schools and community. Founded in 1990, we continue to provide effective and complete safe driving knowledge through the use of technology in our training programmes.

Courses Offered

Class 2

For Class 2A qualified riders who wish to upgrade their licence to ride bigger bike exceeding 400cc.

Class 2A

For Class 2B qualified riders who wish to upgrade their licence to ride bigger bike of equal or not more than 400cc.

Class 2B

For beginners who wish to obtain a driving licence to ride a bike not exceeding 200cc.

Class 3

A Class 3A licence holder is allowed to drive both manual and automatic transmission motor cars of an unladen weight not exceeding 3,000kg with not more than seven passengers (exclusive of the driver).

Class 3A

A Class 3A licence holder is only allowed to drive automatic transmission motor cars without clutch pedals of an unladen weight not exceeding 3,000kg with not more than seven passengers (exclusive of the driver).
